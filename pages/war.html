Tolkien fought in World War 1, eventually having to leave not by his own will, but beacause of a bad case of trench fever. He then went on to use this experience to create compelling characters and story-lines in the Lord of the Rings. Although this isn't necessarily a legend, what it shows me is that one's past, experiences, and knowledge (in a way their own "lore"), has a huge impact on who they are as a person (See the Ents page). This inspired me to create the work you see below.
